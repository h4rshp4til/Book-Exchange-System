About :

The “Book Exchanger” is web based application that provide the facility of exchanging old book to the user. Our community of passionate,generous book-lover is changing the world and touching lives. Our project system will stored seller and buyer information,category details and provide the information. This application purpose is to make it easier for the user to used book exchange to save money,effort in search and buy all these books. Our platform will also provide opportunity to people who normally buy new book but would like to sell them after they have read them.
